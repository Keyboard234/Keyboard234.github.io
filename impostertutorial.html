How To Become The Among Us Imposter

This tutorial will teach you how to become the Imposter from Among Us

What is the Among Us Imposter?

The Among Us Imposter is from the popular video game "Among Us" the Imposter is usually very Sus.

Imposters have to kill Crewmates in order to win and they can do that by killing Crewmates. The Imposter has various abilities such as 'Sabotage' and 'Vent'.

Ok, but how do I become the Imposter?

In order to become the Imposter from Among Us, you have to do a lot of stuff, but it is very effective and worth it!

Find Vents

First, you have to find a Vent. The best types of vent are ones that can support an Among Us Imposter. These vents can be found in many large buildings, so it should not be too hard to find a suitable one.

Obtain Weapons

The Imposter kills Crewmates, so therfore the Imposter from Among Us must have weapons. This is shown in-game in the form of guns, a knife, an alien tongue, or just your hands!

Be Sneaky but also be Sussy

As an Imposter, you must not get caught by the Crewmates or else you will be "Ejected" and you will face the void of Space, so it is very important to be Sneaky. You should not be too Sneaky. You should be just a little bit Sussy so that the Crewmates are scared of your presence, but make sure that they do not know that it is you
